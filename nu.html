Why choose NIIT University


Students at NIIT University get the best academic exposure and industry experience. Read along to know why NU's Programme is the ideal choice for you.

Customisable & Industry linked Curriculum

The curriculum at NU is planned with the right mix of research and industry-relevant subjects taught through project based learning. An example of Industry-linked curriculum is our B Tech CSE curriculum with Digital Transformation Technology content, right from semester one. (Digital Transformation is the new normal in the IT Industry)

Individualised curriculum
One third of credits of the programme can be individualised by the students. NU offers a separate, optional minorcertificate programme that runs parallel to the degree programme. Minors in Finance, Business Analytics and Entrepreneurship are offered to B.Tech students.

Research from 1st year
Undergraduate students are expected to work on an R&D Project - right from the first year. This helps them acquire a research orientation that can be applied in their future jobs.

Credits through co-curricular activities
NU believes that the success of an individual is also dependent on non-academic qualities like collaboration, planning and leadership. To create a holistic personality, academic credits are also given for these activity based courses like Dancing, Music, Photography, etc.

Immersive Industry Practice

During industry practice, a flagship course at NU, a student works on the real-time project of an organisation under the direct mentoring by a faculty and an industry professional. This six-month period makes the transition from a student to an employed professional in industry, seamless and smooth. You can choose from over 519 industry partners at NU.

Talks by Industry Leaders
Corporate leaders from leading organisations regularly come to NIIT University to share their valuable experience. In the current academic year so far, Mr. Som Mittal (Former Chairman - NASSCOM), Mr. Pramod Bhasin (Former CEO - Genpact and known as the Father of Indian BPO Industry), Mr. Rajan Anandan (MD - Google India), Mr. Harpal Singh, Chairman - Save the Children, Mr. Mohit Thukral, Founder - Vivtera and Member - NASSCOM Executive Council, Rajendra Singh (Waterman of India), etc. have visited the campus and had a fruitful interaction with NU students.

Powered by NIIT

NIIT University draws from NIIT’s 37 years of excellence in education and training to offer you the best technology and management programmes.

Startups @ NU

NU provides the necessary ecosystem to encourage students to start their own business ventures. With cutting-edge labs & start-up incubation centre on campus, you can apply your knowledge to real life problems and nurture your entrepreneurial spirit.

Placements

At NU, we have achieved a placement record of 100% for the 2018 batch. Leading companies like IBM, PwC and TCS came to our campus and gave exciting job offers to our students.

Student Clubs and Sports

The campus is always abuzz with various student run clubs. From photography, singing, dancing, theatre, trekking to innovation, there are clubs to meet everyone’s interests. When it comes to sports, NU has indoor sports like chess, carom, billiards and outdoor sports like basketball, cricket, football, tennis - NIIT University also boasts of trekking, valley crossing, zip lining and camping owing to its picturesque setting among the Aravalis.

Ideal Location

The campus location in the pristine Aravalli hills help students learn in a quiet pollution-free atmosphere that is healthy for the mind, body and spirit. Also, being just 90 minutes away from IGI Airport, Delhi is also a major advantage. It’s just like a home away from home.

Seamless community connect

NU aims to bridge the many divides that exist - NU & its rural neighbourhood, campus & its adjoining forest, village & industrial communities, and university and the adjoining Japanese zone.

Conclusion

When you start your educational journey at NU, you don’t just start an academic programme, you actually embrace a new life. A life where you will be mentored, motivated and given an ideal environment to achieve your career dreams. See you at NU!
